1. Overall business performance : 
The total revenue is 15,95,831 generated from 10,000 orders, with an average order value of 160.
Revenue per order is moderate (160) means customers are not spending high per purchase.
Quantity sold (15,504) vs total orders (10,000). It means around 2 items per order (low basket size).

Interpretation : Customers are placing frequent but small size orders.

Action : Interduce combo offers, use cross selling & upselling.


2. Category performance : Revenue is highly concentrated in the Beauty category, contributing majority of the total sales, while Electronics and Clothing are underperform significantly.

Interpretation : Business is over depending on one category and electronics is almost negligible.

Action : Reduce risk by expanding other categories and investigating why electronics is underperforming (demand?/pricing?).


3. Country wise performance : Revenue is concentrated in few key countries like Brazil, Australia, India, Canada, while several regions show relatively low contribution.

Interpretation : Opportunity to scale several regions like South Africa, Japan, Spain these are low performing countries. 

Action : 
Focus marketing on top-performing countries
Analyze low-performing countries (localization issue? / Low demand? / Logistics problem?)


4. Customer segment analysis : Revenue is almost splits between High Value and Low Value customers, While Mid Value customers are contributed less.

Interpretation : Customers are not moving from low to mid and mid to high.

Action : Create strategies to convert low-value customer to mid-value and some targeted offers & loyalty programs.


5. Year wise revenue trend : Revenue shows growth from 2022 to 2023, followed by decline in 2024 and significant drop in 2025.

Interpretation : Business peaked in 2023 and possible decline demands due to operations afterward.

Action : Investigate, market changes?, loss of customers?, or seasonal / incomplete 2025 data?
